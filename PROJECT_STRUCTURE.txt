🚀 QUICK START

1. Setup:
   export TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN='your_token'
   cd "Pi to Youtube"

2. Run:
   yt-agent-env/bin/python main.py telegram

3. Chat di Telegram:
   /start
   Upload /path/video.mp4 judulnya Tutorial
   Edit video123 judul Baru
